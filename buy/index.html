Common questions

How does the license key work?

After purchase on Gumroad, you receive a license key by email. Enter it in TASS when prompted on first launch. The key is tied to your account and can be activated on the number of devices your tier allows.

What happens when my license expires?

When your annual license expires, TASS enters a read-only mode: you can open and export existing projects, but you cannot run new analyses. Renewing restores full access immediately.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. TASS includes a 30-day free trial on first installation with full access to all features. The trial limits imports to 500 rows. No license key or credit card required.

Can I pay using a grant or purchase order?

Yes. Department licenses support invoice-based billing, compatible with most university grant accounts and departmental purchase processes. Contact us to initiate a quote.

Does a license include the T-Lex native dictionaries?

T-Lex dictionaries are developed independently of the TASS license and are freely available to all users for academic and non-commercial research. They are not behind a paywall.

What operating systems does TASS support at launch?

TASS v1.0 launches as a native Windows application (Windows 10 and 11). macOS support is planned for a future release.

What is your refund policy?

If TASS does not work as described on your system, contact us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, no questions asked.
