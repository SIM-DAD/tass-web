Privacy Policy

TASS (Text Analysis Support Software) — SIM DAD LLC — Effective April 12, 2026 — Version 1.0

This Privacy Policy describes how SIM DAD LLC (“SIM DAD,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the TASS desktop application and the TASS website at usetass.app. TASS is a local dictionary-based text analysis desktop app that runs entirely on your machine.

1. Information the Application Collects

TASS processes all text data and analysis entirely on your local machine. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your text data, analysis results, dictionaries, or usage data.

The only information transmitted from the application is during license key activation, which includes:

  • Your email address (as provided during purchase)
  • A device identifier (used to enforce the activation limit)

This activation data is processed by Gumroad on behalf of SIM DAD LLC. No other data leaves your machine through the application.

2. Information the Website Collects

The TASS website at usetass.app may collect the following information:

  • Email address — If you voluntarily subscribe to our waitlist or mailing list, your email address is collected and stored by Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), our email service provider.
  • Analytics data — We use Google Analytics (GA4, measurement ID: G-96N90GBXRX) to understand website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and referral source. Analytics cookies are only set after you provide consent through the cookie banner.

3. No Telemetry or Cloud Processing

TASS does not include any telemetry or usage analytics. All text analysis, dictionary processing, and visualization generation happens 100% locally on your device. No text data or analysis results are ever sent to SIM DAD LLC or any third party. The only data the developer can ever receive is a crash report that you choose to send, described below.

Error reports

TASS does not transmit any data automatically. If TASS encounters an unexpected error, you may be prompted to send a crash report. Crash reports are sent only when you click Send, are addressed to support+tass@simdadllc.com through your own mail client, and contain only the following information:

  • the product name and version
  • the date and time of the crash
  • the technical error (traceback)
  • your operating system and version
  • your CPU model and total RAM
  • (if relevant to the crash) your Python runtime version

Crash reports never contain documents, dictionary contents, analysis data, file paths from your home directory, environment variables, your username, your hostname, your machine identifier, your IP address, your license key, or your order information. File paths inside the technical error are automatically redacted before the report is shown to you. There is no third-party error-tracking service.

You can disable crash-report prompts at any time in Settings → Privacy. You can review or delete saved reports in the same panel.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Gumroad — Payment processing and license key management. Gumroad processes your purchase transaction and manages license activation. Their privacy policy is available at gumroad.com/privacy.
  • Brevo — Email communications. If you subscribe to our mailing list, your email is stored by Brevo. Their privacy policy is available at brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy.
  • Google Analytics — Website analytics only (not in the desktop application). Consent-required. Their privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

License activation data is retained for as long as your license is active. Email addresses collected through the mailing list are retained until you unsubscribe or request removal.

To request deletion of your personal data, contact us at support@simdadllc.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited personal data we collect. Because TASS processes all content locally, your text data and analysis results never leave your device and are protected by your own system’s security.

7. Children’s Privacy

TASS is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at support@simdadllc.com and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Your continued use of TASS after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
