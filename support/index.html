Frequently asked questions

TASS won’t launch after installation. What should I do?

Make sure you are running Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit). Try right-clicking the TASS shortcut and selecting “Run as administrator.” If the issue persists, email us with your Windows version and any error messages you see.

My license key isn’t working.

Check that you are entering the key exactly as it appeared in your Gumroad confirmation email, including any hyphens. An internet connection is required for activation. If you are behind a corporate firewall, check that outbound HTTPS to api.gumroad.com is allowed.

Can I move my license to a new computer?

Yes. Deactivate TASS on the old machine (Settings → License → Deactivate), then activate on the new one. If you no longer have access to the old machine, email us and we will reset your activation count.

How do I update TASS?

Download the latest installer from the download page and run it. Your projects, settings, and license key are preserved across updates.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. TASS processes all text locally on your machine. The only network request is the license key activation check. Your texts, results, and projects are never transmitted.

Does TASS run on Mac or Linux?

TASS v1.0 is Windows-only (Windows 10 and 11). macOS support is planned for a future release.

Known limitations (v1.0)

These are known constraints in the initial release. Many are planned for future versions.

Trial import limit

The 30-day free trial limits imports to 500 rows. Activate a license key to remove this limit.

Windows only at launch

v1.0 ships as a native Windows application. macOS support is in evaluation for a future release.

No SPSS or R export

v1.0 exports CSV, Excel (multi-sheet), and APA plain-text tables. SPSS (.sav) and R (.rds) exports are planned for v1.1.
